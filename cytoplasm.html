The cytoplasm is the fluid found in cells.
All othe organelles are found floating in the cytoplasm.
The cytoplasm of the cell contains enzymes which help break down waste.
The cytoplasm keeps all organelles in place, but the cytoplasm itself is shaped by the cytoskeleton.The cytoplasm also cointains cell nutrients.
Without the cytoplasm, the cell would be deflated, and all the organelles would not be able to keep their position.
Fun Fact:
Ribosomes that make enzymes (Digestive Proteins) float in the cytoplasm, unlike ribosomes that produce proteins to leave the cell, which are found in the Rough ER.
